# Math Edge Cases

Inline: $x^2$

Display:

$$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

Dollar signs that are NOT math: $100 and $200

Price: $50.00 each

Multiple inline: $a$ and $b$ and $c$

Matrix:

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Fraction: $\frac{1}{2}$

Greek: $\alpha + \beta = \gamma$
