# Math Expressions

## Inline Math

The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

Einstein's famous equation: $E = mc^2$.

Big O notation: $O(n \log n)$.

## Display Math

$$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

## Matrix

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
ax + by \\
cx + dy
\end{pmatrix}
$$

## Aligned Equations

$$
\begin{aligned}
f(x) &= x^2 + 2x + 1 \\
     &= (x + 1)^2
\end{aligned}
$$
